## How to Access Your Member Benefits

Access your perks in one location. The member portal is where you can find your membership ID number, digital membership card, and renew or upgrade your membership. When you log in and navigate to the “Member Benefits” tab, you will find instructions to access specific benefits at various locations including using a special promo code or showing your membership card (physical or digital version).

To create your member profile:

1. Visit [the AlumniQ portal](http://umd.alumniq.com/) and select Register Now.
2. Fill out the requested information and select Sign Up.
3. You will receive a verification email to the address you provided. Use that email to instantly set up your account password.

After you submit, your new account will sync with our membership records within two business days.

You will know your account is synced when you see Member Benefits listed in the navigation bar at the top of the page and your current membership will display at the top right corner.
