# Coaches Corner

Your Alumni Association is fearlessly committed to your success. We are here to provide you with resources to enhance your personal and professional goals. Coaches Corner is  a program that aims to connect Terps with access to top-level alum coaches interested in helping you start, advance or pivot your career. Our coaches:

-   Work at various and scalable price points
-   Offer exclusive discounts to Terps like you- _just mention you're a Terp in your first outreach message to unlock exclusive deals_ 
-   Have expertise in various career levels, industries and fields

## Lauren Lefkowitz '98

## Executive Coach

![Lauren Lefkowitz](https://umd-alumni.transforms.svdcdn.com/production/rich-text/Headshot-Square-Medium.png?w=620&h=640&auto=compress%2Cformat&fit=crop&dm=1771017532&s=071bb25443a1fed555567fed13ebb4d3)

**_Fine is a Trap - You Deserve Better_**

**Fine is a Trap** was founded on the principle that no one feels “fine”. As humans, we have much stronger feelings than that, and if we’re saying we’re “fine”, we’re usually avoiding sharing what’s actually difficult. When we get willing to address the “fine”, we have the opportunity to create amazing!

Lauren is an **Executive Coach**, partnering one-on-one with corporate leaders (individually or company-sponsored) ready to overcome burnout, create healthy boundaries, reduce busyness, develop executive skills, elevate or change their careers, and find joy in their work.

In addition, Lauren is a **Fractional Chief HR Officer** for companies with up to 100 employees supporting times of change. She offers strategic advisory services, employee training, organizational development planning, and systems selection and implementation.

She’s also a certified **Laughter Yoga Instructor**, offering virtual employee wellness sessions!

A former corporate Human Resources and Operations executive, Lauren has 25+ years' HR experience in for- and non-profit companies and has been coaching for more than 15 years. She’s managed, consulted with, and coached c-suite and emerging leaders in a variety of departments. Lauren graduated from UMD in 1998 with a BA in Psychology, is a Professional Certified Coach (ICF PCC), a Certified Ontological Executive Coach, and holds Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) and Senior SHRM Professional (SHRM SCP) certifications.

Using strategic thinking, creative solutions, and empathetic humor, she creates trusting and productive relationships.
